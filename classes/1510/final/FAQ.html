Links for learning

MIT Free Course Ware
Audit MIT courses for free with this catalog. Lessons include lecture notes, slides, recordings and full assignments. This is an amazing resource. Recommended courses: Introduction to Algorithms, Elements of Software Construction*, Computer System Engineering, Blockchain and Money, Introduction to Computational Thinking and Data Science*, Software Engineering Concepts. *If you had to only pick one it should be Elements of Software Construction or Introduction to Computational Thinking and Data Science* Both have tons of information and all of the course content is fully uploaded
Youtube Lessons
Above is a link to my favorite youtuber for coding: CodeBro. His ability to casual explain coding concepts makes learning easy. It's like having your friend explain coding to you. He does not go beyond basic concepts but provides a great foundation for anyone wanting to learn. I have to give credit to everything I know to this guy, he really helped me get started.
Baeldung Spring Boot Reference guide
Baeldung is a popular online resource for Spring boot, and backend development. It is a great online resource for when creating spring boot apps. I would not rely on this site to provide the best on programming fundanmentals. This site is best to be used for a refresher of something already known or to quickly close the gaps in something you already kind of know.
The Forage: a job simulator
This site I cannot talk too much about from personal experience. I have yet to follow through with a course from here but plan to in the future. This site provides a "day in the life" style course for multiple compainies to let you get a feel for what the requirements are to thrive in that position. These courses will give you an idea of what you need to focus on in your education.
